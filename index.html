PrepPal - Meal Planning App

PrepPal is a mobile app designed to simplify weekly meal planning and grocery shopping. The project addressed the frustration many users face with disorganized meal prep and forgotten ingredients. Through user interviews and iterative prototyping, we designed a personalized experience that helps users plan meals based on their dietary preferences and auto-generates grocery lists.

SmartFridge App

This is a smart home interface designed for both a fridge touch screen and a companion mobile app. The system helps users track food inventory and reduce waste by syncing data between the fridge and their phone. Users can view what's inside the fridge remotely and get recipe suggestions based on available ingredients. The project focused on designing intuitive, responsive interfaces for two connected platforms.
